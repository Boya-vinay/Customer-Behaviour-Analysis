# Customer Behavior & Purchase Analysis

  

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchase behavior using a dataset of 3,900 customers to uncover actionable business insights.

The analysis focuses on identifying revenue drivers, customer segments, product performance, and growth opportunities to support real-world business decision-making.

## Business Objectives

Understand customer purchasing patterns

Identify high-value customer segments

Analyze impact of discounts, subscriptions, and shipping

Discover top-performing products and categories

Provide strategic recommendations to increase revenue

## Key Insights & Business Decisions

 ## Revenue Drivers

Clothing is the highest revenue-generating category

Young Adults are the top revenue contributors

Revenue is balanced across genders


#### Recommendations :

Focus on product preferences instead of gender-based targeting

## Customer Segmentation

80%+ customers are Loyal Buyers

Very low number of new customers

Repeat buyers are mostly non-subscribers


#### Recommendations :

Increase customer acquisition campaigns

Retain loyal users through rewards programs

## Subscription Analysis

Non-subscribers generate ~3x higher total revenue

Average spending is similar across both groups


#### Recommendations :

Improve subscription value with:

Exclusive discounts

Loyalty perks

Early access to sales

## Discount Strategy

Products like Hat, Sneakers, Coat, Sweater, Pants depend heavily on discounts (~50%)

Discounts can still drive above-average purchases


#### Recommendations :

Use targeted & personalized discounts

Avoid excessive discounting to protect profit margins

## Shipping Behavior

Express shipping users spend more than standard users


#### Recommendations :

Promote express delivery for premium customers

Bundle express shipping with high-value products

## * Product Performance

Top-rated products drive customer satisfaction & repeat purchases

Few products dominate category performance


#### Recommendations :

Promote high-rated products in marketing

Use them for cross-selling & bundling

## Repeat Customer Behavior

Many repeat buyers are not subscribed

#### Recommendations :

Convert repeat buyers into subscribers using milestone-based offers

## Strategic Recommendations

Increase subscription adoption with better benefits

Target Young Adults using focused marketing

Optimize discount strategy using personalization

Invest in new customer acquisition (referrals, offers)

Promote top products & express shipping

## Business Impact

✔ Strong loyal customer base✔ Stable average purchase value✔ High-performing product categories
